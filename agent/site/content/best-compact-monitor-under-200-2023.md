## Buying Guide with Tips

When shopping for a compact monitor, consider the following factors:

1. **Size and Resolution:** Ensure the monitor fits your workspace. A Full HD resolution (1920x1080) is ideal for clarity and detail.
2. **Portability:** If you plan to move the monitor frequently, look for lightweight models with slim profiles.
3. **Connectivity:** Check compatibility with your devices. USB-C is becoming the standard, but HDMI and DisplayPort are also common.
4. **Adjustability:** An adjustable stand can improve comfort and ergonomics, especially for long working hours.
5. **Budget:** Set a budget that aligns with your needs. While more expensive monitors may offer better features, many budget models perform well.
