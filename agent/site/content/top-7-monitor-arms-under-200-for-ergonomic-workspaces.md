## Buying Guide: What to Consider When Choosing a Monitor Arm

When selecting a monitor arm under $200, keep the following factors in mind:

1. **Weight Capacity:** Ensure the arm can support the weight of your monitors. Check the manufacturer's specifications.
2. **Adjustment Features:** Look for arms with tilt, swivel, and height adjustment for optimal ergonomics.
3. **Mounting Options:** Decide between clamp and grommet mounts based on your desk setup.
4. **Compatibility:** Ensure the arm is VESA compatible with your monitor's mounting pattern.
5. **Desk Space:** Consider the size of your desk and how much space the monitor arm will take up.
