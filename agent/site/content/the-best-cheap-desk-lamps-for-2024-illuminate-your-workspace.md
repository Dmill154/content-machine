## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best Cheap Desk Lamp

When selecting a budget-friendly desk lamp, consider the following factors:

1. **Brightness and Color Temperature:** Look for lamps with adjustable brightness and color temperature to suit different tasks and times of day.

2. **Design and Size:** Make sure the lamp fits your desk space and complements your decor. Compact designs are ideal for smaller work areas.

3. **Energy Efficiency:** Opt for LED lamps to save on electricity bills and reduce environmental impact.

4. **Features:** Think about additional features like USB charging ports, touch controls, or smart connectivity if they align with your needs.

5. **Durability:** Check reviews for build quality and durability to ensure your lamp will last.

6. **Price:** Set a budget and compare various options to find the best value for your needs.
