## Buying Guide: Tips for Choosing a Monitor

1. **Screen Size**: Consider how much desk space you have and what size you need for your work. A larger screen can improve productivity but requires more space.
   
2. **Resolution**: For professional tasks, opt for at least Full HD (1920 x 1080) resolution to ensure clear visuals.

3. **Connectivity**: Check the ports available on your devices. HDMI and USB connections are common, so ensure your monitor has compatible options.

4. **Portability**: If you plan to travel with your monitor, look for lightweight, USB-powered options for convenience.

5. **Adjustability**: If ergonomics are important to you, consider monitors with adjustable stands for height and tilt.
