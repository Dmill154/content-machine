## Buying Guide with Tips
- **Consider Your Needs**: Determine what you’ll primarily use the monitor for—gaming, work, or casual browsing. This will help narrow down your choices.
- **Resolution Matters**: For general use, a Full HD (1920x1080) monitor is sufficient, but if you work with graphics, consider higher resolutions.
- **Panel Types**: IPS panels offer better color accuracy and viewing angles, while TN panels can be faster for gaming but may lack color fidelity.
- **Connectivity**: Check for the types of ports available (HDMI, DisplayPort, USB-C) to ensure compatibility with your devices.
- **Size and Ergonomics**: A larger screen can enhance productivity, but ensure it fits your workspace. An adjustable stand is a bonus for ergonomic comfort.
