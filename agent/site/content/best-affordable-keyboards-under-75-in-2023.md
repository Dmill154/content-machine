## Buying Guide with Tips
When purchasing a keyboard, consider the following:

1. **Typing Style**: Are you a heavy typist or a casual user? Your typing habits can influence the type of keyboard you need (mechanical, membrane, ergonomic).
2. **Connectivity**: Decide between wired and wireless. Wireless options offer portability, while wired keyboards often provide a more stable connection.
3. **Ergonomics**: If you type for long periods, look for ergonomic designs that provide wrist support and a comfortable angle.
4. **Backlighting**: If you work in low-light conditions, a keyboard with backlit keys can help improve visibility.
5. **Size and Layout**: Ensure the keyboard size fits your workspace and that the layout suits your typing style.
